Prime example of absolutism: Frankreich
Aim of absolutism: 
	Stabilizing the nation after the devastation of wars
	30 Years' War:
		Mainly a religious war, between catholics, lutherans and calvinists
	The belief that monarchs were appointed by god gave the monarchs further power, cause to unite the nation
	Monarchs also have power over the french church.
	
In france, the main difference between nobles & non-nobles was the difference in standing against law.
